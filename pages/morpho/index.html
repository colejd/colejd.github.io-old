Morpho

Morpho is a creative painting tool which uses simulated chemical reactions to bring your drawings to life. To learn a little more, and to try out something similar, try out the prototype web version here.

Download for macOS

iOS version coming soon!

Features

  • Learn more about the Reaction-Diffusion systems that power Morpho with a built-in wiki

  • Alter the properties of the chemical reactions to create your own look

  • Change the look with color themes

  • Import your own images to play with

Minimum Requirements

  • macOS High Sierra

  • Any Apple computer from 2012 on
