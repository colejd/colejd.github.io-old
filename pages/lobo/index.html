LOBO Buoy Visualizer

This is a visualization of time series analysis of streaming data from LOBO's buoys on the Damariscotta River. They took the buoys out of the river without warning on 11/18/2016, so this program emulates the passage of time over historical data (at an accelerated rate).

For more information, visit UMaine Land/Ocean Biogeochemical Observatory.
